Courses Offered

We provide high level courses to our students
with best study material and regular assesments.

IIT DROPPER

A course of Ascent for 12th pass out students in Maths for the preperation of advance competative exams like Mains And Advance to build up their career.

IIT Fresher

A course of Ascent for the 10th pass out students who aspire to be an engineer.The course is 2 years long with the classroom courses which prepare the student for the boards and the competative exams of Mains and Advance.

PMT DROPPER

A course of Ascent for 12th pass out students in Biology for the preperation of competative exams like AIPMT and AIIMS to build up their career and be a successful Doctor's in their lives.

PMT FRESHER

A course of Ascent for the 10th pass out students who aspire to be a doctor.The course is 2 years long with the classroom courses which prepare the student for the boards and the competative exams like AIPMT and AIIMS.

CRASH COURSES

A course of 2 months which is helpful for the student to recap all the important topics and questions just before the exams.

SUMMER COURSES

The summer course is our yet another short term course which is for the students who want to prepare for the board exams. During this course the student is prepared briefly for the 12th board exams.
